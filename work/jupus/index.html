Booking a priest for a funeral looks like picking a date. Underneath sit rotating on-call rotas, cemetery opening hours, and a grieving family waiting for an answer. ChurchDesk asked me to lead product and design on a system that would keep the surface simple — and become something the rest of the platform could build on, not just funeral booking.

A color engine built for the brand, not the industry default

Bibel TV · 2025 – 2026

Bibel TV has over 10,000 videos in its catalog and a team too small to hand-pick a theme color for each one. A larger operation might do that as part of its artwork pipeline; at Bibel TV's size, it isn't on the table. So the colors have to come from the artwork itself, computed. I built the engine that does it, in OKLCH so every card carries an evenly-weighted theme and the grid reads as a coherent collection in both light and dark mode.
