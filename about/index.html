About

Hands-on design leader with 15+ years of experience building human-centered organizations that create human-centered products.

My Approach

I believe exceptional products come from exceptional teams. As a hands-on design leader, I build the psychological safety, inclusive collaboration, and technical excellence that enables breakthrough user experiences.

Most design leaders choose between strategic thinking and hands-on craft. I do both. I can open Figma and solve your technical problem in the morning, then facilitate a cross-functional alignment session in the afternoon.

Leadership Philosophy

My approach is grounded in a simple belief: innovation starts with dialogue. The same empathy we apply to user research must extend to team collaboration.

The Three Pillars

  • Psychological Safety — The foundation for technical innovation. Teams that feel safe take creative risks.
  • Inclusive Participation — Different minds need different systems. Designing collaboration for cognitive diversity.
  • Dialogue-Based Change — Transformation through conversation, not command. Sustainable change comes from understanding.

Technical Philosophy

I specialize in privacy-first design—creating exceptional user experiences without traditional analytics dependency. Proven across 4+ companies with alternative validation methods.

This isn't a limitation; it's a strategic advantage. Privacy-first design is increasingly valuable as GDPR+ regulations expand globally, and my methodology succeeds in regulated environments like finance, healthcare, and religious data.
